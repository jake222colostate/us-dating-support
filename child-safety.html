Child Safety Standards

RealTake has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). We prohibit any content that exploits, endangers, sexualizes, or depicts minors in a sexual context.

Prohibited content and behavior

  • Any CSAM or content that sexualizes minors.
  • Grooming, sexual solicitation, or exploitation of minors.
  • Any attempt to trade, distribute, or request CSAM.

Detection and enforcement

  • We moderate user-generated photos and content before public display.
  • We investigate reports promptly and take action, including removal and account bans.
  • We preserve relevant information as required for legal compliance and investigations.

Reporting

Users can report safety concerns in-app (for example, reporting a profile, message, or content). If you need to report an urgent child safety issue and cannot access the app, contact us at: jakesherman6@gmail.com.

Cooperation with authorities

We comply with applicable child safety laws and cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities where required.
