Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-14

This Privacy Policy describes how Us Dating (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Us Dating mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and authentication identifiers needed to create and access your account.
  • Profile information: the details you choose to provide (e.g., display name, bio, interests).
  • Content you create: photos, posts, quiz content, messages, and other content you submit in the app.
  • Usage data: app interactions and diagnostic information to help us maintain and improve the Service.
  • Approximate location (if enabled): to support nearby discovery features. You can disable location permissions at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Purchase information (if you subscribe): subscription status and transaction identifiers/receipts needed to provide paid features.

How we use information

  • Provide and operate the Service (accounts, profiles, matching, posts, messaging, and other features).
  • Improve safety and integrity (moderation, fraud prevention, enforcing policies, investigating reports).
  • Customer support and communications (responding to requests and service-related notices).
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve performance (analytics and diagnostics).

How we share information

We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: infrastructure and tooling vendors used to run the Service (e.g., hosting, databases, analytics, payments).
  • Payments: purchases are processed by the app store platform (Apple) and/or payment processors; we receive subscription/receipt data as needed to unlock features.
  • Legal and safety: if required by law or to protect users, the public, and the Service.

Your choices

  • Access and update: you can update your profile information in the app.
  • Permissions: you can control device permissions (like location) in iOS Settings.
  • Delete account: to request account deletion, contact us at support@us-dating.com.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business and safety purposes, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region).

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.
