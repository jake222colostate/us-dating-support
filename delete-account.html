Delete your RealTake account

RealTake lets you request deletion of your account and associated data from inside the app.

How to delete your account (in-app)

  1. Open RealTake and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and account identifiers.
  • Your posts and media associated with your account (where applicable).

What may be retained

We may retain limited information when required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or transaction recordkeeping purposes.

Timeline

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.
